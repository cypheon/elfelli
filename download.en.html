Download

Releases

Releases of Elfelli are available in the Gna! download area of the project.

Development versions

Elfelli uses Subversion (SVN) to manage the source code. To checkout the most current development version of Elfelli, execute the following command:

svn co svn://svn.gna.org/svn/elfelli/trunk elfelli


You can also download the daily development snapshot.
